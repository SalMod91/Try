Story

Lujain is a dentist and a researcher who has a great passion for cooking. After migrating to Switzerland and realizing that cooking is her love language, the idea of this food truck began. It is all about sharing the food she makes and watching people enjoying it. Brewing her grandmother recipes with spices and flavors from all over the world, she is creating the simplest yet most delicious bites. Fresh bread and ingredients are one of her secrets. If want to know more, just come by and she will spell out all her recipes for you.
